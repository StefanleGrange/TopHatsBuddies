About Us

Our mission

The CTU Buddy website aims to provide students with a helpful companion platform to assist them in their personal studies. Our goal is to provide students with a platform to effectively provide course related topics and resources for students who may be struggling with the content. We also aim to provide a collaborative environment for students to ask questions and have their queries answered by students/teachers who understand the topic and provide ways to explain the topic effectively.

About the team

The CTU Buddy development team consists mainly of students who have determined there is a need for students to have effective communication on campus regarding their course topics. The team thus strives to create an easy and convenient platform for students to collaborate and work together to understand their related course content.
